iOS Adventure Games

I spend a lot of time travelling for business which means I have a lot of time to kill. One of my favourite past times is playing adventure games and my iPad has been a godsend. Here are brief reviews of some of the games I’ve played during the long flights.

“Kathy Rain” A thoroughly enjoyable
adventure with a decent story + voice acting and nice art. I completed this
over the course of a couple of flights and while there were plenty of
challenging puzzles I never got completelystuck or frustrated, which is quite
rare.

“The Last Door” I’m not a huge fan
of this style of pixel art, but it works pretty well. The game is atmospheric
with some genuinely tense moments, and I appreciated that they didn’t give
into cheap jump scares. The puzzles were at about the right level of
difficulty for me and I managed to get through it all without resorting to
peeking at any hints.
“The Last Door: Season 2”
In some ways Season 2 improves on the first outing, but overall I found it a
more frustrating affair. The hub like nature of the locations which meant a
lot of traipsing back and forth, with subtle clues being easily missed didn’t
help. There were a couple of - IMO - utterly baffling puzzles which were still
baffling after I had looked up the solutions.. maybe I had had a glass of wine
too many by that point. That said, it was still enjoyable.
