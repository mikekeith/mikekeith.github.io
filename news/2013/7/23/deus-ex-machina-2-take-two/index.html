Deus Ex Machina 2 take two

Posted on 23 July 2013 10:12 NZST by Michael Keith

After an unsuccessful first attempt at a Kickstarter, the Automata folks have solicited feedback and re-launched the campaign with tweaked rewards, logo and even aspects of the game itself. Perhaps more importantly, they’re asking for considerably less. Hopefully this time they’ll make it, though I do wonder why the campaign is only lasting for 2 weeks.
