From Bedrooms to Billions

Posted on 12 March 2013 08:49 NZDT by Michael Keith

Category: Documentaries

After a successful round of crowdfunding last year, the people behind the “From Bedrooms to Billions” documentary and conducting a new Kickstarter campaign in order to cover the myriad of costs involved in finishing and polishing the film. With the passing of Mike Singleton, it’s become clear that we need to record the histories of the people involved in the UK games industry during the 80s. The interviews they’ve managed to snag are extremely impressive - so many of my heroes are in there. Naturally there are some omissions, but this is obviously the product of people who feel passionately about the subject.

They’ve managed to pass their original goal, but the more funding they can raise the more great stuff they can include (archive TV footage and so on).

And if there weren’t already enough reasons to contribute, they’ve got Rob Hubbard and Ben Daglish on board to help with the soundtrack.
