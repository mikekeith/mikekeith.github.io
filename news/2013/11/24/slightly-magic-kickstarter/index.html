Slightly Magic Kickstarter

Posted on 24 November 2013 17:54 NZDT by Michael Keith

Category: Kickstarter

Colin Jones is probably best known for his excellent rock star management simulation, “Rock Star Ate My Hamster”, which featured many thinly veiled (rather biting) pop culture references. “Slightly Magic” was a later creation of his which was always intended to be part of a trilogy of games, but sadly the latter two chapters were never released. Colin is now hoping to resurrect Slightly, starting with an updated version of “Slightly Magic” followed by two new games to complete the series.

The latest Retro Asylum Podcast features a great interview with Colin, who sounds like a really nice bloke. I always find it sad when a talented person leaves the industry, so it’s good to hear when one returns.
