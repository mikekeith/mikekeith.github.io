Spectrum Addict: Load "FILM 2" Kickstarter

Posted on 29 October 2017 17:45 NZDT by Michael Keith

Category: Kickstarter

The Kickstarter for a follow up to the “Memoirs of a Spectrum Addict” documentary, “Load ‘FILM 2’ has entered its final week. The line up of Speccy developers is impressive, including the likes of my old boss Jon Ritman and Bo Jangeborg. As someone who was obsessed by Delta4’s adventure games back in the day, I’m particularly pleased to see Fergus McNeil in there as well.

These documentaries are clearly a labour of love for Andy and I strongly believe that we need to be capturing the stories of these pioneers, so I really hope this funds.
