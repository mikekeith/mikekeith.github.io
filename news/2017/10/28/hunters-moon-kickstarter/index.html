Hunter's Moon Kickstarter

Posted on 28 October 2017 10:35 NZDT by Michael Keith

Category: Kickstarter

The legendary C64 software house Thalamus is back! Created in 1986 by mag publisher Newsfield, it quickly grew a reputation for quality titles. The connection with Zzap!64 probably didn’t hurt either.

News broke a couple of months ago that the label was being reborn as Thalamus Digital, and that we could look forward to some of those classic games making a come back - including the possibility of a new installment of Creatures.

The first title to get the remaster treatment is Hunter’s Moon, the Kickstarter for which is currently entering it’s final hours. The funding goal was breezed past, but it would be great to see some of the stretch goals met!
