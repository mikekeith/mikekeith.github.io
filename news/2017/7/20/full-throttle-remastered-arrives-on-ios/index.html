Full Throttle Remastered arrives on iOS

Posted on 20 July 2017 21:02 NZST by Michael Keith

Category: New Releases

Finally! I’ve been waiting for this one for a while: “Full Throttle Remastered” has been released for iOS. The iPad has become my adventure gaming device of choice (aside from 8bit text adventures, and I’m trying to figure out a solution for that), so it’s great to see another LucasArts classic on the platform.

Not only that, but to coincide with the release Double Fine have slashed the prices of “Grim Fandango Remastered” and “The Day of the Tentacle Remastered” - both highly recommended and absolute bargains at this price.
