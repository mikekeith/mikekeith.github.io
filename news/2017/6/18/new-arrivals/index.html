New Arrivals

Posted on 18 June 2017 17:42 NZST by Michael Keith

Category: New Games

Since getting back into the C64/Amiga scene, one of the things I’ve really loved is that there are still people out there making & publishing games for the machines. So I finally got around to picking up a few. From Psytronkik there is:

  • “Kung Fu Maniacs Trilogy”
  • “Gravitrix”
  • “X-Force”
  • “Space Trip 2086”.

And one from Pond that I had been eagerly awaiting:

  • “The Bear Essentials”

These should keep me busy for a little while…
