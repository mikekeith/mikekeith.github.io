Chaos Reborn Kickstarter Launched

Posted on 18 March 2014 19:51 NZDT by Michael Keith

Category: Kickstarter

After about a year of building a prototype and refining the systems, Julian Gollop has launched a Kickstarter for “Chaos Reborn”. Gollop has created many brilliant games over the years - “Rebelstar”, “Laser Squad” and “X-Com” to name a few - and now he’s returning to one of his first, [“Chaos”][131]. It will be a turn based affair, set in a fantasy realm with magic and unique creatures, and will feature a variety of multiplayer modes.
